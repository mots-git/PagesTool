Vanity URL

Information required for Vanity URL request:

  • Page URL's
  • Confirmation email from admins of both pages involved.

    - Example: "I approve the updating of the vanity url of Test Page (Page ID 12345) from “/pageurl” to “/newpageurl"

  • Is the change small/minimal? Or is this the result of a rebrand?
